About Me

Wilder Molyneux is a tried and true Seattleite, born on Seattle’s Capitol Hill, educated near and far, and loves the Pacific Northwest. He has a passion for full stack web development. He started programming computers at the age of eight and has never stopped his love for technology, computing, and rapid change.


In his day job, he works at a Fortune 50 multinational corporation as an Enterprise Architect. He holds a certificate in Enterprise Architecture from Carnegie Mellon University and is a certified practitioner of The Open Group’s Architecture Framework (TOGAF), which he uses daily.


When not keeping up with technology or working, you can find Wilder outdoors either flying through the air on bicycles, taking two-wheeled backcountry trips with his friends, or camping with his family.
